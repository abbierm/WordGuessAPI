WordGuessAPI

Welcome! WordGuessAPI is a wordle clone you play by making API calls. Ideally this was created to to test and provide a large amount of feedback and data for people who programmed their own wordle solvers.


How To Play Wordle

At the website: #TODO PUT WEBSITE HERE once a day a new 5 letter word is chosen. Users have 6 guesses to try and figure out what the word is. After each guess, the website will display the user's guess with a color coded background based on the correct word. The different colors are:

  • Green : The letter is in the word and in the correct spot
  • Yellow : The letter is in the word but not in the correct spot
  • Grey : The letter is not in the word (or has already been accounted for in another guess spot)


For example, in the screen shot shown above the user took 4 guesses (starting at the word "GREAT") to determine the correct word "PIXIE".

How to play WordGuessAPI

To emulate the color feedback Wordle gives, WordGuessAPI returns a 5 character string with a 'G' (Green), 'Y' (Yellow), or 'B' (Grey/Black). Guess feedback is sent back to the player inside of a nested JSON dictionary. If the Wordle screenshot above was played via WordGuessAPI instead, the feedback would look like: 


{
    "1": {"guess": "GREAT", "feedback": "BBYBB"},
    "2": {"guess": "SLIME", "feedback": "BBYBG"},
    "3": {"guess": "INDIE", "feedback": "YBBGG"},
    "4": {"guess": "PIXIE", "feedback": "GGGGG"}
}
